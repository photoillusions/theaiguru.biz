Tony is the founder of TheAIGuru.biz — a one-person, one-machine AI development practice based in Mt. Holly, New Jersey.

From Camera to Code

For over a decade I've been the photographer behind Photo Illusions — capturing portraits, events, and brand work for clients across the East Coast. Somewhere along the way, automation became its own art form.

What started as scripts to batch-edit thousands of images turned into custom desktop CRMs, AI-powered image pipelines, full booking systems, and 24/7 voice agents that book appointments while I sleep.

TheAIGuru.biz is the AI division of that work — a place where small businesses can get the same kind of bespoke automation that used to cost six figures, built and shipped fast by someone who actually codes the systems himself.
